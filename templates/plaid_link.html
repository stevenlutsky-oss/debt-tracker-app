🔗 Link Your Bank Account

Connect your bank to automatically sync credit card balances.

How it works:

  1. Click "Link Account" below to open Plaid
  2. Log in with your bank credentials (sandbox/test credentials)
  3. Select a credit card account to link
  4. Your balance will automatically sync!

🔒 Your credentials are never stored on our server. Plaid handles all bank authentication securely.

Why use Plaid?

  • ✅ Automatic balance updates - never manually enter balances again
  • ✅ Real-time data - see your current balance, not last month's statement
  • ✅ Secure - bank-level encryption via Plaid
  • ✅ Free in sandbox mode - test with fake accounts
