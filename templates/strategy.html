💡 How It Works

Snowball

  • List debts from smallest to largest
  • Make minimum payments on all except smallest
  • Put extra money toward the smallest debt
  • When smallest is paid, roll that payment to the next
  • Pros: Quick wins, stays motivated
  • Cons: May pay more interest over time

Avalanche

  • List debts from highest to lowest interest rate
  • Make minimum payments on all except highest rate
  • Put extra money toward highest interest debt
  • When highest is paid, roll that payment to the next
  • Pros: Saves the most money
  • Cons: May take longer to see progress
